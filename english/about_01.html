About Us

Who We Are

Healthcare Plus Co., Ltd. was founded in 2017, have been separated from Essence Plus. Distributed the high quality healthcare raw materials, including API (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients), Topical Excipients, Silicone Adhesives and Tubing. We are concentrating on providing the diversified and reliable product line in the wide range of healthcare application. At Healthcare Plus, we take pride in all the valuable knowledge and our prompt after-sale services to win customers’ trust.
